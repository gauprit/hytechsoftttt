PDF Merger

Combine multiple PDF files into a single document. All processing happens in your browser — files never leave your device.

Tip: Select files in the order you want them merged.

How PDF merging works

  • Select two or more PDF files from your device.
  • We load and combine them directly in your browser.
  • You get a single merged PDF file to download.
  • No server upload, full privacy.

Frequently asked questions

Will compression reduce my PDF quality?
Compression may lower image resolution. Use "High Quality" if preservation is important.
Are my files uploaded to a server?
No — everything runs in your browser for privacy.
What if the result is still large?
Try the "Smallest File" option or split large PDFs and compress separately.
